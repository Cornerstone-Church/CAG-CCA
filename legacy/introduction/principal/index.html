  • Quarter 3 Update

    Greetings families,
    Third quarter for CCA was filled with so many incredible experiences - Spirit Week, Black History Month presentations, a Field Trip to the Frederick Douglass House, a parent and staff vs student basketball game, and even a student art showcase that began at the Bowie Town Center and ended with a student receiving a proclamation from the Mayor of Bowie. We are truly grateful for all of the amazing things God is doing in, with, and through our students and staff.

    However, as the third quarter ended for CCA, our staff and students were saddened to learn that we would not be able to return to our building due to the impact of the world-wide health crisis caused by COVID-19. We, along with our country's entire educational system, made the needed transition to adopt new distance-learning modules in order to continue the learning process for our students. More importantly, we took comfort in knowing that our God is sovereign and always in control. While we may not understand why we are facing this pandemic, we know that God is faithful in His love for us.

    As a school, we are praying for the health and comfort of those who have been impacted by this virus. We are praying for the strength and encouragement of all first-responders and those workers who continue to serve others during this time. We pray for wisdom at all levels of leadership. We pray for perseverance and protection over the lives of missionaries serving around the world. We pray that every follower of Christ will continue to obey His calling to be a light in this world.

    On Tuesday, March 17, CCA raised the American flag and the Christian flag for the first time on a new flagpole planted outside of our building. Even though the building is temporarily closed, as these symbols now wave outsâ€‹ide our school, may our love for Christ and others never be contained to a building. May our light now shine even brighter. Make Jesus proud CCA!

    Our next "virtual" open house is scheduled for Tuesday, April 21. Families interested in enrolling at CCA for the 2020-2021 school year may register for this event by sending an e-mail to dalston@cag.org

    Blessings,

  • Quarter 1 Update


    Parents, staff and community partners interact at CCA's Back-To-School Night Event

    Greetings Families,
    Praise God! CCA is off to a great start for the 2019-2020 school year. On September 12, we hosted our Back-to-School night and started our program by giving everything back to Jesus through worship. I'm so grateful for our small yet power-filled and ever-increasing worship team. The evening was filled with much to see and do. Not only did parents have an opportunity to learn more about the grade level curriculum materials from the teachers, but they also met many of our community partners who offered food, sports, art space, music instruction, college/career/scholarship instruction, and family counseling services. Prizes were won, book drawings were held, and many parents signed-up to get involved in school life at CCA. What a fun and enjoyable evening. Thank you parents and community partners for attending!

    Our next open house for perspective students will be held on Tuesday, October 8 from 7pm-8pm. Parents may register for this event by calling 301-262-7683 by October 7. I look forward to meeting new faces for next year!

    Principal

  • Summer Update #2


    As we prepare for the new school year, our summer staff reminds everyone of God's word and puts the finishing touches on CCA bulletin boards!

    Greetings CCA families,
    God is so good! Let's all give thanks for His faithfulness. Just take a moment to reflect on His faithfulness in your life today and share that testimony with your children. I pray that we can take the time to "Reflect" His face to His children each day.

    With the start of the school year soon approaching, our God has been faithful in so many ways: From growing our staff, to updating our facilities; from expanding community partnerships to enhancing our technology curriculum, excitement abounds in all He has done and all He has in store.

    Since you've already heard a little bit about the great work our building service team has been doing around the school, let me share some new details about CCA's enhanced technology curriculum for this year. Not only will students in our primary grades have access to computers, and gain instruction in exercising safe computer use, but they will also have opportunities to demonstrate proper keyboarding techniques and practice producing graphics while building basic scripts and loops. Upper elementary students will have opportunities to design publications using digital media and present researched information effectively to an audience. More information about our technology enhancements will be shared at the Aug 22 student orientation.

    The first day of school is August 26! Please join the CCA staff in prayer for our school year on Saturday, August 24 at 7pm in the Cornerstone sanctuary.

    Do enjoy these last days of summer. I look forward to seeing everyone soon!

    Daryl Alston
    Principal
    Cornerstone Christian Academy

  • Summer 2019

    Greetings CCA Families,

    I am greatly honored and humbled to serve as your principal. Cornerstone Christian Academy is a truly special school that has provided a wonderful spiritual and academic foundation for each of my own children. Now, I am excited to partner with each of you and your children as they begin to walk through and discover God's magnificent plan for their lives.

    While CCA already provides outlets for effective communication, I hope to use this forum as an additional means of updating you on quarterly activities. But, the start of these summer months have given me time to both reflect upon God's faithfulness and prepare for the work He is about to do.

    I reflect upon the amazing work that was done through VBS. Over 500 children joined us this summer for Vacation Bible School as the CCA classrooms were "powered up" and transformed into fun video game themes to help remind everyone that His true power lives within us. The move of the Holy Spirit was awesome and I was so glad to see, meet, and work beside the many CCA students, parents, and staff members who attended.

    Preparations allow me to see an exciting future on the horizon for CCA. While renovation projects, professional development courses, website updates, curriculum planning, and classroom re-paintings occur throughout the building over these next two months, I know that even much greater changes are in store for us as we diligently seek His face. May His will always be above any plans for CCA.

    As we continue through the summer months, I pray for each family's safety and continued growth in Him. I pray that we will all strive in one accord to reflect Christ in our words and deeds. And, as this is the summer, I pray that we all take some time to break routines and enjoy a new wonder of His immense creation with families. I look forward to meeting you in August and hearing all about our students' summer activities!

    To keep the lines of communication open, feel free to contact me by e-mail at dalston@cag.org with any questions, comments, or concerns. CCA summer hours are 9am -3pm Monday -Thursday and 9am-12 noon on Friday. Please call or e-mail ahead for an appointment.

    Thankful for all those helping us prepare for the new school year with painting and remodeling at CCA:

    Have a wonderful summer!

    Daryl Alston
    Principal
    Cornerstone Christian Academy
