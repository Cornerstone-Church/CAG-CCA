Biblical Resources

With Games for Students

CCA students must always have adult permission, supervision, and guidance when using the internet.

The following sites have been compiled because they contain free optional educational tools and resources for students that can support their spiritual and academic growth. As the content is not owned or continually monitored by the school, these sites cannot be fully endorsed by CCA. With wisdom, families should determine the best ways in which these available tools can aid in their children’s growth.

Adventure Bible

https://www.adventurebible.com

Bible games and activities get kids engaged and excited about God’s Word including Bible coloring pages, Bible lessons for kids, Bible maps, devotions for kids, Bible verses for kids, and Bible trivia.

Answers in Genesis

https://answersingenesis.org/kids

Answers in Genesis is an apologetics ministry, dedicated to enabling Christians to defend their faith and to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ effectively.
