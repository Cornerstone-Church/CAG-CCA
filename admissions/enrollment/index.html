Admission Policies

Application is made with testimony of both parents. One parent must be a born-again, Bible-believing, practicing Christian and actively involved in a church body. A non-believing spouse must agree not to oppose the Biblical teaching of CCA. The following items are needed for admittance:

  • Pastoral Recommendation
  • Assessment testing
  • Administrative interviews
  • Parent Agreement signed

All new students are placed on probation for their initial 9 weeks of school. (See Withdrawal Policy)

Pre-K4: Following the State of Maryland guidelines, children admitted to the Pre-Kindergarten program shall be 4 years old on or before September 15, in the year in which they are applying, and developmentally ready.

Kindergarten: Following the State of Maryland guidelines, children admitted to the Kindergarten program will be 5 years old on or before September 15 of the school year in which they are applying. All new Kindergarten students must pass the Kindergarten Readiness Test.

1st through 5th Grade: Assessment tests are administered to students in 1st through 5th grade to determine grade placement.
